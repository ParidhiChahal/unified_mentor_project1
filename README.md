# nassau-candy-profitability-analysis

📊 Nassau Candy Profitability & Cost Analysis Dashboard

This project focuses on analyzing product-level profitability, cost efficiency, and division-wise financial performance for Nassau Candy Distributor. The goal is to move beyond sales volume and uncover true profit drivers using data-driven insights.

# Project Objective

Many high-selling products do not necessarily generate high profits. This project identifies:

High-revenue but low-margin products
Top profit-contributing products
Division-wise financial efficiency
Cost-heavy and margin-risk products

It helps in making informed decisions related to pricing, sourcing, and product portfolio optimization.

# Key Features
📌 Product-level profitability analysis
📌 Gross margin & profit per unit calculations
📌 Division performance comparison (Revenue vs Profit)
📌 Cost vs Sales diagnostic analysis
📌 Pareto (80/20) profit concentration analysis
📌 Identification of low-margin risk products

#Tech Stack
Python (Pandas, NumPy)
Streamlit (Dashboard Development)
Matplotlib (Visualization)
Power BI (Optional for advanced dashboards)

# Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:

Sales, Cost, and Gross Profit
Product and Division details
Customer location and shipping data
Units sold and order-level transactions

# Key KPIs
Gross Margin (%) = Gross Profit ÷ Sales
Profit per Unit = Gross Profit ÷ Units
Revenue Contribution
Profit Contribution

# Insights Generated
A small percentage of products contribute to the majority of profit (Pareto Principle)
Some high-sales products have low margins, indicating pricing inefficiencies
Certain divisions generate high revenue but lower profitability
Cost-heavy products impact overall financial performance

# Business Recommendations
Optimize pricing strategies for low-margin products
Reduce manufacturing or sourcing costs
Focus on high-margin products for growth
Eliminate or redesign underperforming products
Reduce dependency on limited high-profit items
